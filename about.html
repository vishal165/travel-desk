About the studio

Small team, big love for slow travel.

Who we are

Travel Diaries is a tiny travel studio based in India. We started by planning trips for friends who didn’t have the time to obsess over logistics, but still wanted something more personal than a package tour.

Today, we design a limited number of journeys every month, focusing on homestays, local food and flexible itineraries. We’re not chasing volume – we’re chasing well-rested, happy travellers.

Every trip is planned by a real human. We listen, suggest, adjust and keep the conversation honest and transparent.

What we care about

  • Supporting local hosts, guides and small businesses
  • Travelling at a pace that respects both travellers and places
  • Creating room for serendipity, not just checklists
  • Keeping our footprint lighter – smaller groups, slower routes

How we choose partners

We work with a network of hosts and on-ground partners across different regions. Most are small family-run homestays or independent guides.

Locally rooted

We favour people who live in the region and know its stories, seasons and small details.

Guest-first mindset

Clean stays, simple but thoughtful food, clear communication and realistic expectations.

Ethical practices

No wildlife exploitation, no tokenism, and fair compensation for everyone involved.
