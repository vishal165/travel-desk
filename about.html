About the studio

Small team, big love for slow travel.

We’re planners, researchers, and on-ground fixers with one shared goal — to make travel across India feel personal, respectful, and refreshingly human.

Who we are

Travel Diaries started as a favour for friends who wanted something more nuanced than an off-the-shelf tour. Years later, we still limit the number of journeys we design so each itinerary gets proper attention.

You’ll always speak to the humans crafting your trip. We listen, suggest, adjust, and keep the conversation honest from first call to post-trip debrief.

What we care about

  • Supporting local hosts, guides and small businesses
  • Travelling at a pace that respects both travellers and places
  • Creating room for serendipity, not just checklists
  • Keeping our footprint lighter – smaller groups, slower routes
On the road We personally scout every partner before recommending them.

Partner network

How we choose people on the ground.

We work with family-run homestays, naturalists, drivers, and storytellers who share our ethos.

Locally rooted

We favour people who live in the region and know its stories, seasons and small details.

Guest-first mindset

Clean stays, thoughtful food, and honest communication set the tone for every trip.

Ethical practices

No wildlife exploitation, no tokenism, and fair compensation for everyone involved.

Timeline

The Travel Diaries journey so far.

  • 2018 — Passion project

    Weekend planning help for friends travelling to Kumaon and Konkan.

  • 2020 — Studio mode

    Formalised as a tiny studio, focusing on homestays and road trips during reopenings.

  • 2023 — Partner network

    Built a roster of guides, chefs, drivers, and artisans across 10 regions.

  • 2025 — Today

    Hybrid team between Pune, Bengaluru, and on-ground hosts handling only 8–10 trips a month.
