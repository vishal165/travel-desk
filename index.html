Welcome to India

A land of contrasts ready for unhurried journeys.

Snowy Himalayan passes, the arid Thar desert, lush tropical Kerala, and coastlines that run for days. India unwraps millennia of history — the cradle of Buddhism, yoga, algebra, Ayurveda, and storytelling traditions.

We keep itineraries unhurried so you can take in local life, festival rhythms, and slow-travel stays.

Best season?

  • Monsoon magic (Jul–Sep) for lush greens and waterfalls.
  • Winter wonderland (Oct–Feb) for festivals and city strolls.
  • Summer delight (Mar–Jun) for beaches and treks.
  • Spring awakening (Mar–May) for floral valleys and tea gardens.

Questions

Answers we share often.

Is it safe to travel to India?

Take the usual precautions; we work with vetted partners and stay one call away during the trip.

Do I need a visa?

Yes — e-Tourist visas cover most stays under 30 days. We’ll guide you through the application.

Should I get travel insurance?

Highly recommended. We can point you to providers covering medical needs and baggage.

When is the best time to visit?

India is year-round; we’ll align routes with the weather and festivals you’re chasing.
